Home Decor Tip #1: Use the same color, but different textures

If you find yourself having a hard time with decorating your room and picking different matching colors, this is a good tip for you. If you know you like one color, then pick things that are in that color but have different textures, patterns, and are made of different fabrics. It's a super simple way to decoratr without having to think too hard about which colors go together.
